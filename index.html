My Philosophy

I’m a big advocate for right plant, right place. When you give plants the correct growing conditions they flourish and put on a beautiful show with minimal interference.

My aim is to promote gardening practices that respect and enhance the natural world and the creatures living in it. I believe in working with nature, not against it. This means:

  • Organic Gardening: No harmful chemicals, only natural fertilisers and pest control.
  • Biodiversity: Planting a variety of species to support local wildlife and pollinators.
  • Sustainability: Practices like composting, mulching and water conservation.

Join me in creating beautiful, sustainable gardens that bring joy and benefit wildlife.
